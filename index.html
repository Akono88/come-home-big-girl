Full Daily Routine

From the Big Girl Care Guide. Follow this exact timing for stability.

☀️ Morning — 7:00 to 8:00 AM
1. Wake Up & First OutingOpen crate → let her straight outside through back door. Just a couple minutes for bathroom.
2. Prepare & Serve BreakfastWhile she's outside: ~2.5 cups rice & chicken, heated, in bowl. Filtered water in separate bowl. Open back door — hold food & water so she sees them — walk to crate, place both inside, close crate door. Let her eat in peace.
3. Post-Meal WalkWait until done. Remove leftover food & water (never leave standing). Let her outside 15–20 min. For walk: shock collar + leash on → turn right to park → leash off at park, collar setting ≤ 7. Light tap if she drifts.
4. Back Inside — Crate RestRemove shock collar → store in garage with leash. Crate with stuffed animal. Window OPEN (daytime). "Hey Siri, turn on white noise." Close crate door.
🌤️ Midday — 1:00 to 2:00 PM
No meal — hydration & exercise onlyGive filtered water (remove when done). Walk to park — same collar/leash process. Return → remove collar, store in garage.
Free Roam TimePut up metal gate, let her free-roam inside. Nothing out she could chew on. Bathroom door closed.
🌇 Evening — 6:00 to 8:00 PM
1. Evening OutingLet her out in backyard for bathroom break.
2. Serve DinnerSame as morning: ~2.5 cups rice & chicken, heated, in crate with filtered water. Close crate, let her eat.
3. Post-Dinner OutingRemove leftover food & water. Let her out in backyard one more time.
🌙 Bedtime
4. Settle In for the NightPlace in crate with stuffed animal. Close window. Close & lock crate door. "Hey Siri, turn on white noise." Confirm: no standing food, no standing water, bathroom door closed, office door closed.
